Investment Thesis

Arbitraging Innovation Inefficiencies

We capitalize on the fundamental disconnect between value creation—where emerging market institutes excel—and value capture—where they face structural barriers. This is efficiency arbitrage, not disruption.

01

Structural Advantage

US corporate entity provides global IP licensing, VC access, legal protection, and international credibility that Brazilian entities cannot easily replicate.

02

Incentive Arbitrage

Brazilian innovation laws (Lei de Informática, EMBRAPII) fund R&D while we handle commercialization—capturing value without bearing full development costs.

03

Operational Efficiency

"Dollarized" operations create 12-15% cost advantages through optimized tax structures and service-export models for Brazilian talent.
